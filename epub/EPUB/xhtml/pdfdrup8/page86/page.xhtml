Descriptive Paragraph

 

It is one where the sentences written work together to describe something (place, thing or idea) or

 

someone (person, animal, fantastic creature, etc.). Read the following example:

 

“Sunrise is the time of day when the sun appears after a calm darkness. There are

 

different colors in the sky like blue, pink, and purple, mixed and combined. The

 

clouds look sometimes like pieces of cotton or doodles in the sky. The sun shows

 

its powerful rays, while the moon hides shy and sleepy in the sky. People get ready

 

for a brand new day, new surprises and opportunities. There is excitement, hope

 

and energy in the air, something new is just about to begin!”
